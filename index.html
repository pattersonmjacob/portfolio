Approach

I align stakeholders around performance goals, design decision-rich practice, and validate results with clear business-facing metrics.

Design principles

  • Design for the behavior change required on the job, not just knowledge recall.
  • Use scenario pressure and immediate feedback to build judgment under real constraints.
  • Keep UX friction low so learners spend effort on decisions, not navigation.

Typical deliverables

  • Performance analysis, learner profile, and success metrics framework.
  • Storyboard, decision map, script, and interactive prototype.
  • SCORM/web production build with accessibility and QA pass.
  • Evaluation plan: completion, assessment quality, time-to-competency, and field KPI movement.

Capabilities

I bridge learning strategy and production craft: from stakeholder alignment to polished, measurable delivery.

Tools and execution

  • HTML/CSS/JS for web-based modules, prototypes, and interaction shells.
  • Figma for UX flows, component systems, and stakeholder-ready design walkthroughs.

Strategy and measurement

  • Performance objectives, assessment design, and practice calibration.
  • Analytics planning for completion, confidence lift, accuracy, and speed-to-proficiency.
  • Executive-ready impact narratives that connect learning outputs to business outcomes.
